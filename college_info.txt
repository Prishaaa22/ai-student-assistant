RNS First Grade College (RNSFGC) – Information
About the College

RNS First Grade College (RNSFGC) was established in 2012 and was founded by industrialist and philanthropist Dr. R. N. Shetty. The college recently received autonomous status, giving it academic flexibility to update the curriculum and syllabus regularly.
The institution is accredited by NAAC with an ‘A’ grade.
RNSFGC focuses on value-driven education, experiential learning, skill development, and holistic student growth. It aims to blend academics with practical exposure, extracurricular activities, sports, cultural programs, and personality development.

Vision, Mission & Core Values

RNSFGC emphasizes the following values and goals:

Academic Excellence & Integrity: High educational standards with ethical values.

Holistic Personal Development: Focus on leadership, communication, values, discipline, and social responsibility.

Bridging Theory & Practice: Practical exposure through labs, projects, and industry-relevant curriculum.

Career & Future Readiness: Placement training, skill-development programs, and professional preparedness.

Inclusive Growth: Respect, ethical behavior, cultural heritage, and responsible citizenship.

The mission of the college is to deliver quality education while developing well-rounded individuals prepared for global, professional, and societal challenges.

Courses Offered / Programs

Undergraduate Programs (UG):

BCA – Bachelor of Computer Applications
Focus: Programming, databases, networking, data science, AI/ML, cloud computing, and emerging technologies.

B.Com – Bachelor of Commerce
Focus: Business, accounting, finance, economics, and corporate skills.

BBA – Bachelor of Business Administration
Focus: Management, business fundamentals, leadership, and entrepreneurship.

Postgraduate Program (PG):

MBA – Master of Business Administration
Focus: Advanced business and management studies (introduced recently in 2023).

Departments

Department of Computer Applications (BCA)
Focuses on IT, programming, cloud, AI/ML, data science, and networking.

Department of Commerce (B.Com)
Focuses on business, commerce, accounting, finance, and entrepreneurship skills.

Department of Management (BBA / MBA)
Focuses on management studies, leadership, and business administration.

Facilities & Campus Infrastructure

Computer Labs & IT Infrastructure: Equipped with modern systems for programming, IT, and business studies.

Library & Digital Library: Books, journals, e-resources, previous papers, and reference materials.

Hostel Facility: Available for outstation students.

Auditorium & Seminar Halls: Used for seminars, cultural events, and academic programs.

Sports & Recreation: Grounds for football, volleyball, kabaddi, badminton, indoor games, and a fitness center/gym.

Canteen: Provides meals and refreshments for students and staff.

ICT-enabled Classrooms: More than 22 classrooms with smart boards and digital learning tools.
